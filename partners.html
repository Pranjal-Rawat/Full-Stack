Our Partners

At Pukaar, we are proud to collaborate with esteemed organizations that share our commitment to making a positive impact.

Our partners include
Gyanoday, dedicated to providing quality education and empowering communities
UCOST, which focuses on promoting science and technology for sustainable development
Giri Foundation, known for its environmental conservation efforts
Himaagaman Foundation, which works towards rural development and women's empowerment
Project Aaina, which strives to uplift underprivileged sections of society through various social initiatives.
Together, we work towards creating a better future and driving meaningful change.
